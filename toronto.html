Toronto's must-skate

the bentway

The meeting point for our group skates, and the heart of the Toronto Inline community.
The Bentway skate rink is smooth and sheltered, making it a go to for skate sessions.

Closest go train station: Exhibition go

harbour front trail

The trail is a dream ride- flat, scenic and breezy. With lake ontario on one side and the skyline on the other, it’s one of the best blading routes in the city.

Ashbridge Skate Park

The city's largest skatepark with something for everyone.

sankofa square (formerly dundas square)

Located in Toronto's busiest intersection, the vibrant section makes for an iconic roll through.

college park

Smooth skate rink and open space in the heart of downtown. A popular meet up spot for local skaters.

riverdale park

Push up the hill, roll down fast, and finish with one of the best skyline views Toronto has to offer. Riverdale Park is a classic skate destination for both the ride and the view.

Rebel Lookout Point

Cruise by the lake and stop at Rebel Lookout for sweeping waterfront and skyline views. It’s a nighttime favorite for skaters who want a scenic cool-down.Push up the hill, roll down fast, and finish with one of the best skyline views Toronto has to offer.

Nathan Philipps Square

Smooth surfaces and iconic backdrops make Nathan Phillips Square a must-skate stop. The Toronto sign and City Hall set the scene for group photos and city vibes on wheels.
